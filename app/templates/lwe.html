How to use the CVP/LWE module
  • Click on Recalculate keys and look at the plot where you will see the parameters with error.
  • On the left you can see the LWE equation.
Note that we have limited the modulus to be a small prime, such that the plot won't be too large.
Sometimes the parameters will not be sufficiently large to see the error, hence why the algorithms are performed in many dimensions.
